## Data Deletion Request

To delete your user data collected through Facebook OAuth, please follow these steps:

### What Data is Collected

When you sign in using Facebook OAuth, we collect:

- Your Facebook user name
- Your Facebook user ID
- Your Facebook profile picture

We do **not** collect your email address or any other personal information.

### How to Request Deletion

To have your data deleted, please send an email to **me@ryuichirosuzuki.com** with the following information:

1. **Subject line**: User Data Deletion Request
2. **Include in the email**:
   - Your Facebook user name
   - Clear statement that you want your data deleted

### Deletion Timeline

Once we receive your deletion request, your data will be permanently deleted within **3 business days**.

You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

### Questions?

If you have any questions about this process, please contact me@ryuichirosuzuki.com.
